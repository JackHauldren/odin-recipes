Easy Ham

Description

An easy crockpot ham anybody can make. Requires little effort other than time.

Ingredients

  • Any ham that will fit inside a crockpot
  • Honey
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Place ham in crockpot. Fill crockpot with water until ham is mostly covered
  2. Put crockpot on "low" heat
  3. Drizzle a smackledorf of honey on top
  4. Dust honey with brown sugar
  6. This is the ham's new home for 1/2 to 1 day
  7. Every couple of hours, or when you remember, reapply honey and brown sugar to taste
  8. After 12 to 24 hours, the ham will be ready!
