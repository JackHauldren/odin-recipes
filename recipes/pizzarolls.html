Faux Pizza Rolls

8 9

Description

10

Pizza Rolls that are quick, dirty, and more importantly, require no culinary skills. Pillsbury approved.

11

Ingredients

12
    13
  • Pillsbury flaky cresent rolls. Get the big tube. Or offbrand, I'm not your dad.
    • 14
  • Any cheese you prefer. I recommend pepperjack and mozzeralla.
    • 15
  • Pepperoni
  • Optionally, marinara sauce
    • 16
17

Steps

18
    19
  1. Break open your crescent roll tube and seperate pieces
    2. 20
  2. These rolls will be filled, so stretch them out into larger triangles
    3. 21
  3. Put your toppings on the bottom of the side you'd roll if these were normal crescent rolls. I recommend Cheese-Pep-Cheese-Pep
    4. 22
  4. Roll it up like a normal crescent roll, being careful not to split the crust
  5. 23
  6. Tuck corners into edges. Avoid having any openings in your cholesteral lump, you don't want melted cheese all over your pan/oven
    7. 24
  7. Remember you need to preheat the oven. Curse yourself for not doing it before step 1. We're going to use whatever heat is on the tube
    8. 25
  8. Grease your pan, put your pizza rolls on pan. Stick in oven for time recommended on tube. Even though they're larger, the default time is fine
  9. Enjoy!
    10. 26
