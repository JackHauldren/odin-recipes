"Homemade" Mozzarella Sticks

Description

Totally homemade, and totally delicious, mozzarella sticks. You just can't beat home cookin'.

Ingredients

  • One box of frozen mozzarella sticks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to degrees shown on the box. Leave box outside freezer while oven is preheating.
  2. When oven is preheated, grease pan and place sticks on pan. Place in oven for two minutes more than shown on box.
  3. Enjoy!
